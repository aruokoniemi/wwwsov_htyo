How to play

objective

The objective of the game is to pass through all the checkpoints.

In singleplayer mode, the checkpoints are always in the same positions and there are a total of 13 checkpoints. Try to finish the race as fast as possible!

In multiplayer mode, the checkpoints are randomized. the winner is decided by who finishes the race in the fastest time.

Movement

Use the arrow keys to move.

drift mode

pressing spacebar starts drift mode.

in drift mode, the car rotates when you press the left and right keys. after releasing spacebar, or after 2 seconds are up, the drift will release.

released drifts reward you with boosted acceleration and a higher maximum velocity. the effect depends on the change in your car's angle, and will last for maximum of 2 seconds.

environmental effects

sand will slow you down and negate any effects gained from a successful drift.

hitting the railings will deal damage to your car. taking damage decreases your maximum velocity. taking too much damage will crash your car.

driving into water will crash you instantly.

statistics

statistics are recorded for logged in players for both single- and multiplayer games.
